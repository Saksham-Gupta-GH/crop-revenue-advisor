About AgriSmart

AgriSmart is a machine learning-based crop recommendation system that helps users choose the most profitable crop for a given district, season, and land area.

The system is built using real-world agricultural datasets, including crop production, rainfall statistics, and market price data. These datasets are cleaned, merged, and processed to calculate yield and revenue patterns across India.

A stacking classification model (Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and AdaBoost) is used to predict the best crop, while a Gradient Boosting regression model estimates the expected revenue per hectare.

The trained models are saved and directly used in this application, enabling fast, real-time predictions without retraining.

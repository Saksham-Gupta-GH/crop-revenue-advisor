The Technology

AgriSmart is a predictive tool designed to assist in agricultural planning by leveraging historical data and machine learning.

Methodology

Our system analyzes district-level production history, seasonal rainfall variations, and market pricing to identify the most suitable crops for a given area.

The Models

We utilize a combination of ensemble classification and regression models to provide both crop recommendations and revenue estimations.

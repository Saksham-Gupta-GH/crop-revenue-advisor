The Science Behind AgriSmart

How we use data to empower agricultural decisions.

The Vision

AgriSmart was built to solve a fundamental problem: crop selection risk. By combining decades of historical yield data, seasonal rainfall patterns, and market price volatility, we provide a mathematical foundation for what to plant next.

Predictive Pipeline

Our system uses a dual-engine approach:

  • Stacking Ensemble: We use a multi-layer classifier that weighs predictions from Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and AdaBoost models to suggest the best-fitting crop.
  • Revenue Regression: A secondary model estimates revenue potential based on regional historical yield and adjusted market price indices.

Data Integrity

We source data from official agricultural records, meteorology departments, and wholesale market boards. All data is cleaned and normalized before being used to train the models stored in this application.
